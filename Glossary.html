[FR: Exporter]

Fiscal Year

[FR: ]

G

Gross Domestic Product (GDP)

[FR: Produit Intérieur Brut (PIB)]

GDP measures the monetary value of final goods and services—that is, those that are bought by the final user—produced in a country in a given period of time FROM Goal 15, 16, 24


Greenhouse Gas (GHG)

[FR: émissions de gaz à effet de serre]

emissions of gas that contribute to the greenhouse effect by absorbing infrared radiation, e.g., carbon dioxide and chlorofluorocarbons. From Goal 27: GHG

Peak

[FR: Optimale]

Peak refers to the optimal value for the associated goal (not necessarily highest value). From ALL goals.


PSE Attainment Rate

[[FR: taux de réussite aux études postsecondaires]

Attainment Rate is the percentage of people with a certain level of education completed. Note, this is not a measure for the number of graduates per year more like the number of degree/diploma holders per year. FROM Goal 10.

Recession

Revenue Recycling

[FR: ]

Revenue recycling refers to mechanisms through which income generated from carbon taxation is earmarked and returned back to society. From Goal 27:GHG


R&D Per Capita

[FR: recherche et développement par habitant]

R&D per capita is essentially the expenditure on research and development divided by the number of habitants in New Brunswick. FROM goal 11.
